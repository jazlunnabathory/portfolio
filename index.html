ABOUT ME

Hey there! I'm Jazmin Reyes, but you can call me Jaz. Currently, I'm a Full Developer Student, diving into the vast and exciting world of technology.

I'm originally from El Salvador, my journey into technology began in high school.

I hold certifications in Web Page Layout and Landing Page for business, along with proficiency in Excel.

Currently, I'm proudly contributing to a Virtual Assistant Company, with plans to kick off a Bachelor's in Technology and Business Management next year.

Beyond the coding screens, I'm passionate about life. I love food, travel, I love horror movies, and listening to music. Oh, did I mention my love for dying my hair purple?

My work philosophy is simple: Always look forward and never give up. I love learning and I love helping others by sharing knowledge.

I want to be part of this new stage where women are making a difference in technology.

I want you to feel at ease, connect with my experiences, and be inspired to reach out. Let's collaborate on projects and make things happen!
